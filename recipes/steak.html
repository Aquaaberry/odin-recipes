Ingredients

  • Flat iron steak
  • Olive oil
  • Minced garlic
  • Chopped fresh parsley
  • Chopped fresh rosemary
  • Chopped fresh chives
  • Cabernet Sauvignon
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Dry mustard powder

Step By Step

  1. Place steak in large resealable bag.
  2. Stir ingredients together in small bowl.
  3. Pour marinade into bag with steak and refrigerate for 2 to 3 hours.
  4. Heat nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Sear and cook steak for 3-4 minutes each side.
  6. Allow steaks to rest for about 5 minutes before serving.
  7. Serve
