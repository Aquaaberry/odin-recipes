Steakhouse Steak

Description

Imagine sinking your teeth into a perfectly cooked steak, a culinary masterpiece that tantalizes the senses with its rich aroma and mouthwatering flavor. This succulent steak, cooked to your preferred level of doneness, boasts a charred exterior that gives way to a tender and juicy center. Each savory bite is a symphony of tastes, as the natural umami of the beef is elevated by a subtle blend of herbs and spices. Whether it's a classic ribeye, a tender filet mignon, or a hearty New York strip, the steak is impeccably seasoned, enhancing its inherent flavor without overpowering it. Served alongside are indulgent accompaniments such as creamy mashed potatoes, buttery roasted vegetables, or a velvety mushroom sauce, each element adding depth and richness to the dish. With every mouthful, you're transported to a realm of culinary bliss, savoring the perfection of a truly delicious steak.

Ingredients

  • Flat iron steak
  • Olive oil
  • Minced garlic
  • Chopped fresh parsley
  • Chopped fresh rosemary
  • Chopped fresh chives
  • Cabernet Sauvignon
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Dry mustard powder
